My Story

Started as a mother.
Grew into a mission.

My journey into natural, remedy-based wellness began in 2020 — not in a clinic, but in my own home, with my own children. As a mother of three girls, I was constantly researching everyday products, gentle remedies, and lifestyle choices to make sure my family was using what was truly safe and supportive.

That journey led me to complete my Licentiate in Homeopathy through the College of Practical Homeopathy in the United Kingdom. What started as a personal mission became a calling to help other families do the same.

I understand the overwhelm of trying to make better choices in a world full of conflicting information. My work is about making natural living accessible, practical, and grounded in real family life — because I live it myself every single day.

I am not registered with the AHPCSA in South Africa. I hold a Licentiate in Homeopathy in the United Kingdom. All sessions and content are for educational and wellness support only and do not replace professional medical care.
